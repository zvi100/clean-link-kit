# Code of Conduct

Clean Link Kit is a small public project. Everyone should be able to ask questions, report bugs, and suggest changes without feeling unwelcome.

## Our standards

- Be respectful and patient.
- Assume good intent.
- Give clear feedback without personal attacks.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.
- Help new contributors understand what to do next.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or insults.
- Discrimination of any kind.
- Sharing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic or disruptive comments.

## Reporting

If something feels unsafe or inappropriate, contact the maintainers through GitHub. Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block users when needed to keep the project healthy.
